We serve a wide range of drinks including wines, lagers, ciders, real ales & cocktails. Our Lagers & Ciders include Estrella Galicia, Birra Moretti, Level Head IPA and Aspalls Cider with a range of bottled ciders & lagers. Our Real Ales include "Flower Pots Brewery","Stonehenge Ales", "Triple FFF Brewery" & "Hopback Brewery" as regular offerings at the bar.

We have carefully selected some excellent wines to pair with our exquisite menu dishes and some quality house wines by the glass.

We are dog-friendly but request all dogs kept on a lead, indoors and outdoors.
We have pet rabbits in the garden who are someitmes able to be seen when not too shy, we ask you to be quiet and respectful around them as not to scare them.

Our Menus

Our menus are designed with families in mind, packed with fresh seasonal flavours and locally sourced ingredients, so every meal is wholesome, delicious, and perfect for sharing together.

We serve food all day Wednesday to Saturday from 12pm. Our Lunch Menu is 12-6pm and the A la Carte is 12-3pm and 6-9pm. We also have a "Bar Snacks" menu too, available all day. See our opening hours for further details.

Our Rooms

Our rooms are tastefully decorated incorporating the different elements of our property. The Inn Rooms are in the older part of the main building dating back 200 years in parts and incorporate king size beds and en-suite bathrooms. The Garden Rooms are modern, spacious and incorporate super king beds with walk-in showers in the en-suites.
All rooms have televisions, tea/coffee making facilities and spring water.
